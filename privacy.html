Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

Simple Summary: Bubbles collects ZERO data. We don't track you, we don't store anything, and we don't share anything. Your privacy is 100% protected.

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Bubbles ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data Collection

Bubbles does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data whatsoever.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Performance data
  • Game scores or statistics
  • Any other type of data

How the App Works

Bubbles runs entirely on your device. All game activity, including:

  • Bubble drawing and animations
  • Game scores
  • Power-up usage

...happens locally on your iPhone or iPad and is never transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

Bubbles does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services

Internet Connection

Bubbles does not require an internet connection to function and does not make any network requests.

Children's Privacy

Bubbles is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we collect no data whatsoever, there are no privacy concerns related to children's use of the app. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Data Storage

The only data stored is your local high score in the Bubble Dodge game, which is saved on your device using iOS's standard UserDefaults system. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us
  • Is deleted if you uninstall the app

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to:

  • Access
  • Modify
  • Delete
  • Export

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (for example, if we add optional features that require data), we will update this policy and notify users through an app update with release notes.

Compliance

Bubbles complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines

Compliance is simple: we collect nothing, so there's nothing to protect, breach, or misuse.
