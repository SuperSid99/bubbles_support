About the App

Bubbles is a relaxing and fun iOS app that lets you create mesmerizing bubble animations with your fingertips. Whether you want to relax with free-form drawing or challenge yourself with an exciting game, Bubbles offers a clean, minimalist experience.

Features

Free Canvas Mode

Draw and create endless bubbles on a black canvas. Perfect for relaxation and stress relief.

Bubble Dodge Game

Test your reflexes by dodging colorful bubbles. Features exciting power-ups including:

  • Shield: Protect yourself from one hit
  • Slow-Mo: Slow down time to dodge more easily
  • Shrink: Make yourself smaller to fit through tight spaces
  • Ghost Mode: Phase through bubbles temporarily

How to Use

Getting Started

When you open Bubbles, you'll see the main menu with an interactive bubble drawing background. Simply tap anywhere to create bubbles!

Canvas Mode

  1. Tap "Canvas" from the main menu
  2. Touch and drag your finger across the screen to create bubbles
  3. Watch as they float and pop naturally
  4. Tap "Back" to return to the menu

Bubble Dodge Game

  1. Tap "Play Bubble Dodge" from the main menu
  2. Touch and hold the screen to control your bubble
  3. Avoid the falling bubbles to survive
  4. Collect power-ups to gain special abilities
  5. Try to beat your high score!
